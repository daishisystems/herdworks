Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first farm?

After signing in:

  1. Tap the Home tab at the bottom
  2. Tap "Manage Farms" from the quick actions
  3. Tap the "+" button (top right)
  4. Fill in your farm details (name, breed, location, etc.)
  5. Tap "Save"

How do I record breeding events?

To track mating activity:

  1. Go to Home → "All Breeding Events"
  2. Tap the "+" button
  3. Select your farm and lambing season group
  4. Enter breeding details (number of ewes, dates, mating type)
  5. Tap "Save"

How do I add scanning results?

After pregnancy scanning:

  1. Go to Home → "All Scanning Events"
  2. Tap the "+" button
  3. Select your farm and group
  4. Enter scanning data (ewes scanned, singles, twins, triplets, dry)
  5. Tap "Save"

How do I view benchmark comparisons?

To compare your farm's performance with industry standards:

  1. Go to Home tab
  2. Scroll to "Performance Dashboard"
  3. Tap a farm and lambing season group
  4. View metrics like scanning %, lambing %, marking %
  5. Tap any metric to see detailed breakdown

Can I use HerdWorks offline?

Yes! HerdWorks works offline with cached data. Your changes will sync automatically when you reconnect to the internet. The app caches up to 48 MB of data locally for offline access.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. HerdWorks uses:

  • Firebase Authentication - Secure email/password login
  • HTTPS/TLS Encryption - All data transmitted securely
  • Cloud Firestore - Your data stored on Google Cloud servers
  • Private by Default - Only you can access your farm data

Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

Account Management

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot Password"
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Check your email for a password reset link
  4. Follow the link to create a new password

How do I delete my account?

To permanently delete your account and all data:

  1. Open HerdWorks and sign in
  2. Go to the Profile tab (bottom right)
  3. Scroll to the bottom
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm deletion (this cannot be undone)
⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your farms, breeding events, scanning results, and lambing records will be permanently deleted.

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing paul@nexair.io with your account email address.

How do I change my language preference?

HerdWorks supports English and Afrikaans:

  1. Go to the Settings tab
  2. Tap "Language"
  3. Select your preferred language

Technical Requirements

What devices are supported?

  • Platform: iOS (iPhone and iPad)
  • Minimum iOS Version: iOS 17.0 or later
  • Recommended: iOS 18.0 or later for best performance
  • Devices: iPhone SE (3rd gen) and newer, all iPad models with iOS 17+

Do I need internet connection?

An internet connection is required for:

  • Initial sign-in and account setup
  • Syncing data to the cloud
  • Viewing benchmark comparisons

Once data is cached, you can view and edit data offline. Changes will sync when you reconnect.

How much storage does HerdWorks use?

HerdWorks uses minimal storage:

  • App Size: ~10-15 MB
  • Offline Cache: Up to 48 MB (configurable)
  • Total: ~25-65 MB depending on your data volume

Troubleshooting

The app won't sync my data

If your data isn't syncing:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Make sure you're signed in (check Profile tab)
  3. Close and reopen the app
  4. If issue persists, contact support

I can't sign in

If you're having trouble signing in:

  1. Make sure you're using the correct email and password
  2. Check for typos (email addresses are case-sensitive)
  3. Try the "Forgot Password" option to reset
  4. Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  5. Contact support if you still can't access your account

The app crashes or freezes

If HerdWorks is crashing:

  1. Make sure you're running the latest version of iOS
  2. Update HerdWorks to the latest version (App Store)
  3. Restart your device
  4. Clear the app cache (sign out and back in)
  5. Contact support with details about when the crash occurs

My benchmark data isn't showing

Benchmark comparisons require:

  • Complete breeding event data
  • Scanning results for the same group
  • Lambing records to calculate metrics
  • Internet connection to fetch industry benchmarks

Make sure you've entered all required data for your lambing season group.
