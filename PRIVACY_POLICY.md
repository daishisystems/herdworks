# Privacy Policy for HerdWorks

**Last Updated:** January 19, 2025
**Effective Date:** January 19, 2025

## Introduction

HerdWorks ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using HerdWorks, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

When you create an account and use HerdWorks, we collect the following personal information:

- **Email Address:** Used for account authentication and communication
- **First Name and Last Name:** Used to personalize your experience
- **Phone Number:** Used for account verification and communication
- **Personal Address:** Including street address, city, province, and postal code
- **User ID:** A unique identifier assigned to your account

### 2. Farm and Business Information

To provide farm management functionality, we collect:

- **Farm Details:** Farm name, location (city, province, street address, postal code), company name (optional), and farm size
- **Livestock Data:** Sheep breed information, number of production ewes
- **Production Records:**
  - Breeding events (dates, number of ewes, ram details)
  - Scanning results (ultrasound pregnancy scan data)
  - Lambing records (birth outcomes, lamb counts)
- **Performance Metrics:** Calculated statistics based on your production data

### 3. Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information:

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data:** App features accessed, interaction patterns
- **Authentication Tokens:** Secure tokens for maintaining your logged-in session
- **Cache Data:** Local device storage for offline functionality (up to 192 MB)

### 4. Information We Do NOT Collect

HerdWorks does NOT collect:

- Location data (GPS coordinates)
- Camera or photo library access (unless explicitly granted for future features)
- Contacts or calendar information
- Payment or financial information
- Advertising identifiers

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Primary Functions
- **Account Management:** Create, maintain, and secure your user account
- **App Functionality:** Enable core features including farm management, breeding records, scanning events, and lambing records
- **Data Storage:** Store your farm data securely in the cloud with automatic synchronization
- **Offline Access:** Provide offline functionality through local device caching
- **Benchmarking:** Compare your farm's performance against anonymized industry benchmarks

### Communication
- **Service Updates:** Notify you of important app updates or changes
- **Account Security:** Alert you to security-related issues
- **Support:** Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

### Analytics and Improvements
- **Performance Monitoring:** Monitor app performance and identify technical issues
- **Feature Development:** Understand how features are used to improve the app
- **Bug Fixes:** Diagnose and resolve technical problems

## Data Storage and Security

### Firebase Backend

HerdWorks uses Google Firebase services to store and process your data:

- **Firebase Authentication:** Manages user accounts and authentication
- **Cloud Firestore:** Stores your farm and production data
- **Data Location:** Your data is stored on Google Cloud servers in the United States
- **Offline Persistence:** Data is cached locally on your device (up to 192 MB) for offline access

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

- **Encryption:** All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **Authentication:** Secure email/password authentication with Firebase Auth
- **Access Control:** Your data is accessible only to you through your authenticated account
- **Device Security:** Local cache data is protected by iOS device encryption
- **No Third-Party Sharing:** We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes

### Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** Your data is retained for as long as your account remains active
- **Account Deletion:** When you delete your account, all personal information and farm data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days
- **Cached Data:** Local device cache is cleared when you delete the app

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do NOT Sell Your Data

HerdWorks does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

1. **Service Providers:** Google Firebase (as our backend infrastructure provider) processes your data on our behalf under strict data protection agreements
2. **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation
3. **Business Transfer:** In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred (you will be notified in advance)
4. **With Your Consent:** We may share information for other purposes with your explicit consent

### Anonymized Benchmarking

For industry benchmark features:

- Your farm's production data is used to calculate anonymized industry averages
- All identifying information (name, location, user ID) is removed before aggregation
- Benchmark data is calculated from multiple farms to ensure anonymity
- Individual farm data cannot be reverse-engineered from benchmark statistics

### Data Transfers

HerdWorks is operated from South Africa, but data is stored on Google Cloud servers in the United States. By using the app, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States, which may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

### GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

- **Legal Basis:** We process your data based on your consent and for the performance of our contract with you
- **Right to Object:** You can object to data processing for legitimate interests
- **Right to Restriction:** You can request restriction of processing under certain circumstances
- **Right to Lodge Complaint:** You can file a complaint with your local data protection authority

### POPIA Compliance (South African Users)

HerdWorks complies with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA):

- **Lawful Processing:** We process your information lawfully and transparently
- **Purpose Specification:** Data is collected only for specified, lawful purposes
- **Data Minimization:** We collect only the minimum data necessary for app functionality
- **Your Rights:** You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal information
